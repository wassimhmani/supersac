About

BAPLAST was founded in 1992 by Mr Fayçal BRADAI. The company is one of the leading manufacturers of plastic and biodegradable bags in Tunisia. Our products are made of LDPE, HDPE and biodegradable materials and can be produced with transparent or colored ﬁlm. Our technics of extruding, printing, cutting, welding allow the manufacturing of diversiﬁed types of bags that fulﬁll your demand and expectations. In 2017, BAPLAST performed a green shift in order to follow the national strategy for the sustainable development of natural resources and the protection of the environment. Our company took the initiative to convert itself into the production of reusable and compostable bags made from natural resources. .
